# Launch Runbook Overview

## Pre-Launch

Confirm sign-offs; verify hosting; ensure analytics enabled; content freeze

Prepare onboarding materials for internal teams

## Launch Day

Publish Tier 1–Tier 3; announce with internal comms; provide support channels

## Post-Launch (90 days)

Monitor analytics; collect user feedback; address issues within 72 hours

Publish Tier 4 content as planned; run monthly health check-ins

## Rollback/Recovery

If major issue arises, revert to previous stable build; notify stakeholders;
perform post-mortem
