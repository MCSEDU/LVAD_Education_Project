## Phase 1: Soft Launch (July 1–14, 2026)

### Target Users
- VAD Coordinators (all sites)
- Lead VAD Nurses (ICU, step-down)
- Program Directors

### Goals
- Validate content accuracy in clinical context
- Identify UX friction points
- Test quiz engine scoring and completion tracking
- Verify accessibility on hospital-issued devices

### Feedback Collection
- In-app feedback widget (5-question survey per module)
- Weekly 30-minute sync with soft launch cohort
- Bug tracker: GitHub Issues with `soft-launch` label

## Phase 2: Full Launch (July 15, 2026)

### Rollout Order
| Week | Target Group | Method |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Week 1 | Tier 1 — New LVAD patients at implant education |Pre-op education session integration |
| Week 2 | Tier 2 — All cardiac ICU and step-down nurses | Mandatory education email + manager briefing |
| Week 3 | Tier 3 — All APPs in heart failure program | Grand rounds presentation + direct enrollment |
| Week 4 | Tier 4 — Attending physicians, surgeons | CME credit announcement + department heads |

### Communication Plan
- Email announcement from CMO/CNO
- Intranet banner on employee portal
- QR code posters in cardiac units
- Patient-facing: inclusion in discharge education packet

## Phase 3: Monitoring & Maintenance (Ongoing)

### Monthly Metrics Review
- [ ] Module completion rates by tier
- [ ] Quiz pass/fail rates (flag modules <70% pass rate)
- [ ] Time-on-page analytics
- [ ] User-reported errors or outdated content flags
- [ ] New user enrollment vs. completion funnel

### Quarterly Content Review
- [ ] Review against updated device IFU (Instructions for Use)
- [ ] Incorporate new ISHLT guidelines if published
- [ ] Update device-specific content (HeartMate 3 software updates)
- [ ] Review adverse event reports for educational gaps

### Annual Full Content Audit
- Clinical content review by VAD team and program director
- Patient advisory input for Tier 1 content
- Regulatory compliance check (HIPAA, FERPA for health education)
- Accessibility re-audit (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum)
- Platform dependency updates (Node, React, hosting)
