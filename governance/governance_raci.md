# RACI

- **PM**: Accountable for process
- **CL**: Responsible for content strategy and drafting
- **MR**: Consulted for clinical accuracy
- **LCL**: Consulted for compliance
- **UXD**: Responsible for UX/UI and accessibility
- **Dev**: Responsible for implementation
- **QA**: Responsible for testing
- **DAL**: Consulted for analytics
- **CM**: Responsible for rollout
